Queues


Standard:
Data structure where insertion and deletion occur on opposite ends
Follows FIFO ordering
Main methods:
queue.add() adds to bottom of queue
queue.peek() returns head element without removing
queue.poll() returns and removes head of queue
Access: O(N), Search: O(N), Insertion: O(1), Deletion: O(1), Space: O(N)
Applications: graph traversal (breadth-first), when data received asynchronously
